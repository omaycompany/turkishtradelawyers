Comprehensive Legal Services for International Businesses

Market Entry & Business Setup

We assist with Turkish company formation, joint ventures, and market entry strategy. From registering subsidiaries to obtaining licenses and permits, we handle all incorporation steps to ensure your expansion into Turkey is built on a rock-solid legal foundation.

Contracts & Commercial Transactions

Secure your deals with our contract law expertise. We draft, review, and negotiate bilingual English-Turkish contracts for sales, distribution, services, and partnerships. Every agreement is aligned with your business goals and protected under local law.

Regulatory Compliance & Consulting

Stay fully compliant with Turkey's evolving regulations. We provide ongoing compliance consulting, conduct legal audits, and develop training programs for your teams. We ensure you adapt swiftly to new legislation and avoid costly fines.

Dispute Resolution & Legal Representation

Should conflicts arise, we are your dedicated representatives in Turkey. We handle business disputes, lawsuits, and enforcement actions in Turkish courts and arbitration tribunals. Available 24/7 for urgent legal matters.

Localization & Ongoing Legal Support

Think of us as your external in-house counsel in Turkey. We provide continuous legal support for day-to-day matters like employment contracts, HR policies, corporate governance, and regulatory filings as your business evolves.

Training & Compliance Programs

We organize custom training programs for your staff on critical legal topics including anti-corruption, data protection (GDPR/KVKK), and workplace regulations. We help prevent legal issues before they arise.
